Days 61–90 • Prove with Real Work

Scout

Use the contract to clarify use case, data dependency, evaluation, adoption owner, and roadmap decision.

Tagging

Make exception categories, first-pass quality, cycle time, rework, and ownership visible.

Analytics

Identify one repeated customer decision that can move from custom analysis toward a repeatable offering.

Internal AI

Demonstrate one workflow where capacity is released, human authority is preserved, and freed time is redeployed.

Day-90 Leadership Readout

  • What is clearer now?
  • Which bottleneck was removed?
  • Which initiative changed priority based on evidence?
  • What capacity was freed or protected?
  • What should be standardized next?

Operating Principles

Lightweight, not loose

Use the fewest fields and meetings required to make a better decision.

Revenue-prioritized, not revenue-only

Protect data trust, adoption, and platform leverage that create durable economics.

AI inside the work

Redesign workflows around context, authority, exceptions, evaluation, and adoption.

Evidence changes allocation

A metric matters only when it can accelerate, sequence, repair, or stop work.

Six-Month Bridge

Extend the load review into clearer cross-functional execution tracking across Scout, tagging, and analytics; reduce friction; improve visibility into timelines and blockers; and show early AI-enabled capacity gains.

Twelve-Month Bridge

Establish repeatable operational systems, predictable scaling of Scout and tagging initiatives, greater executive focus, and disciplined growth.
