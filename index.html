The Strategy Loadframe

Every initiative should carry its own operating truth.

A lightweight initiative contract connects the customer decision, business economics, accountable owner, dependencies, capacity burden, trust conditions, evidence standard, and next portfolio decision.

First 120 days

Install clarity before adding ceremony.

Start by reading the system, then make work visible, prove the cadence on live priorities, and transfer a durable operating mechanism to the leadership team.

Days 1–30

Read the system

Map commitments, owners, decision rights, economic assumptions, dependencies, and current operating rhythms.

Days 31–60

Make it visible

Stand up the initiative loadframe, a decision log, dependency map, and executive portfolio view.

Days 61–90

Prove the cadence

Run the mechanism across Scout, tagging, analytics, and one internal AI efficiency initiative.

Days 91–120

Compound the system

Refine thresholds, transfer ownership, and turn repeated decisions into institutional learning.
